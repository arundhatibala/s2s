A UNIQUE
CHANCE.

S2S Ventures is entirely run by students. We don’t just teach investing, we offer hands-on experience in leading a VC fund. From developing investment strategies to managing a startup portfolio, our student team takes on real responsibility and makes key decisions. If you're a student in Switzerland with a passion for startups or venture capital and thrive on the freedom to create, S2S Ventures is where you belong!
