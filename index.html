S2S: STUDENTS TO STUDENTS.

S2S Ventures is a fully student-led VC fund from Switzerland, backed by some of the country’s most successful entrepreneurs. We provide student founders with a first check of CHF 25,000 to kickstart their startups and work closely with them to raise their pre-seed round. Our team mirrors the founders we support—students, aspiring investors, and entrepreneurs, eager to drive our fund to new heights.
